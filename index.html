My Life

Hello, my name is Patrick Wright, and I'm a native North Carolinian, born and raised in Greensboro. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2012 and am currently pursusing a master's degree in Mass Communication, with a focus in public interest communications, at the University of Florida.

I love working out and playing video games in my spare time. As a former middle school trumpet player, I'm also looking to get back into making music. In fact, I recently started learning the guitar and can't wait to play and sing songs for my family.

One day, I hope to lead a communications department for an organization supporting important social causes. I want to make a positive impact through effective communications and memorable storytelling.

I developed this website using the Bootstrap CSS framework. This is one of the many tools I learned to use over the course of this semester in MMC5277 Web Design Principles at the Florida. I am excited to take what I have learned over the course of this semester and apply it to my everyday work!
